Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information about what is white water rafting and why the user should consider it as an exciting and fun outdoor activity. We need them to get curious and want to go into a white water rafting rabbit hole so thye do research on the topic and want to do it even more.

Audience

The targeted audience are young adults and risk takers who enjoy the outdoors a lot and people who love to socialize. These people wont need a large income, they just need a small amount of cash and physical attributes as they are white water rafting. They are gonna want ammendities close by like snacks and vending machines. The needs we will have to meet is basic ammendities panflips about white water rafting and other outdoor activities, saftey stuff like life jackets, we will also need mentors, young mentors who know what they are doing as it will attract more customers. They will access this website through their labtop when at home and when they are on the go they will use their mobile devices so this has to be a mobile friendly website.
